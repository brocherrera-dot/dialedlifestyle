Common questions

How it works.

Do I need to live somewhere sunny?

No. Most of my clients are in cities. The circadian scheduler adapts to your latitude and season — even a glass-walled NYC apartment can produce a real morning anchor with the right routine.

What if I cannot afford grass-fed everything?

The grocery calculator builds two parallel lists — Whole Foods retail and Costco bulk — so you can hit the same protein and produce targets at half the cost.

Will I have to take a hundred supplements?

No. Detox protocols open drainage first, then layer binders only when you are pooping daily and sweating regularly. You will likely take fewer pills by month three than month one.

Do you replace my doctor?

Never. I work alongside your physician, especially on labs and Rx-only support. Coaching is the daily execution layer your doctor does not have time for.
